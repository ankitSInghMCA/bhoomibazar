Privacy Policy of Bhumibazar

Effective Date: 10 April 2025

Last Updated: 10 April 2025

At Bhumibazar Propex Pvt. Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "Company", "we", "us", or "our"), trading/doing business via name "bhumibazar", we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and manage your personal data when you access or use our website, mobile application, and services (collectively, the "Platform").

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us
  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email, profile photo, gender, age, address, etc.
  • Registration Data: When you register as a broker/agent/client/visitor on our Platform.
  • Property Listings: Details, images, documents, prices, location, etc.
  • Subscription & Payment Info: Information related to subscription plans (payments handled by third-party providers).
  • User Communications: Calls, messages, emails, chats, feedback, and queries.
  • Platform Interactions: Includes property listings, document uploads, and communications to facilitate transactions.
B. Systematically Collected Data
  • Device & Technical Data: IP address, device ID, OS, browser type, network, location.
  • Usage Data: Includes behavioral data such as browsing patterns and session activities for product and service enhancements.
  • Cookies and Tracking: Enables customization and user experience improvements through tracking technologies.
C. Information from Third Parties
  • Ad partners, analytics providers, marketing agencies, and SSO/third-party login services.

2. Utilization of Data

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To streamline account creation, management, and service provision.
  • To facilitate professional interactions among brokers, agents, and clients.
  • To promote and display property listings while ensuring alignment with platform policies.
  • For lead generation and marketing purposes.
  • To conduct analytical studies to improve marketing, customer engagement, and operational strategies.
  • For subscription, billing, support services, improve and develop our Platform and offerings.
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations and meet statutory and regulatory obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Other Users: For purposes directly tied to the functioning of our platform (e.g., property transactions).
  • Marketing Partners: Includes third-party contractors supporting technical, operational, and marketing processes.
  • Payment Gateways: For subscription processing (no financial data is stored by us).
  • Legal Authorities: As required by law, court order, or government request.
  • Service Providers: For technical, operational, and customer support.
  • In case of Merger/Acquisition: User data may be transferred to the new entity.

4. User Rights and Responsibilities

While users may request access or updates to their data by contacting our Privacy Team, the Company emphasizes mutual responsibility in ensuring data security, such as maintaining strong passwords. Withdrawal of consent may impact the availability of certain services.

5. Data Retention

The Company employs industry-standard mechanisms, including SSL encryption, access controls, and real-time monitoring, to secure data. Retention periods are defined based on operational and legal requirements.

6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices including:

  • SSL encryption
  • Firewalls and server security
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular audits and monitoring

Despite best efforts, no platform is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and manage your account confidentiality.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Third-Party Services

Our Platform may contain links to third-party websites/apps. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Please review their policies before using those services.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze platform performance
  • Serve personalized ads

You can manage cookies via your browser settings. Some features may not work without cookies enabled.

10. Updates to This Policy

The Company reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy periodically. Notifications of material updates will be disseminated as deemed appropriate.
